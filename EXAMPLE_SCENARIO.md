
# Example Scenario: "The Eco-Tech Pitch"

## 1. Strategist
**User** pastes a raw blog post about a new solar panel technology.
**AI** analyzes text and generates:
- **Tone**: "Futuristic, Clean, Green"
- **Keywords**: "Solar", "Efficiency", "Silicon"
- **Visual Features**: "Glass panels", "Sun rays", "Circuit boards"
**User** reviews the generated Outline. It looks good.
**User** clicks "Approve Stage".

## 2. Art Department
**User** sees a Brief populated with concepts like "A close up of a glowing silicon wafer".
**User** clicks "Generate All".
**System** generates 10 images.
**User** deletes 2 weird-looking ones.
**User** clicks "Remove Background" on a specific solar panel icon.
**User** clicks "Approve Assets".

## 3. Architect
**System** unlocks Stage 3.
**User** selects Slide 1 ("Introduction").
**User** drags a "Green Gradient" background asset to the `background` zone.
**User** drags the "Solar Panel" transparent stamp to the `East` zone.
**User** clicks "Suggest Layout" for Slide 2 to let AI decide.
**User** clicks "Approve Layouts".

## 4. Copywriter
**System** unlocks Stage 4.
**User** sees the slide with the background and stamp.
**User** clicks "Generate Copy".
**AI** fills in the Headline: "The Future is Bright".
**User** edits the headline to add a newline for better spacing.
**User** clicks "Approve Copy".

## 5. Publisher
**System** unlocks Stage 5.
**User** turns on "Film Grain" for a cinematic look.
**User** scrolls through the final deck.
**User** clicks "Export PDF".
**System** renders the DOM to a PDF file and downloads it.
